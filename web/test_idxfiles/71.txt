<h2><a href="http://www.nytimes.com/2011/12/03/business/economy/us-adds-120000-jobs-unemployment-drops-to-8-6.html?ref=global-home">
Signs of Hope in Jobs Report; Unemployment Drops to 8.6%</a></h2>
<h6 class="byline">
By CATHERINE RAMPELL			<span class="timestamp">11:06 AM ET</span>
</h6>
<p class="summary">
The Labor Department said payrolls grew by 120,000 in November, a new indicator of economic resilience, but the work force contracted.</p>
